# Image Generation Prompts

Concept visualization only; not fabrication authority.

## Hero Prompt

Photorealistic studio product photo of a small Andean duct-flute family prototype set: a G4 pinkullo-style fipple flute as the main piece, with related tarka and small duct-flute bodies nearby. Show simple cylindrical PVC proof-of-tune material alongside a warmer hardwood or bamboo follow-up blank, a removable block, rectangular fipple window, crisp labium edge, and undersized tone holes ready for controlled tuning. Clean neutral studio background, soft directional lighting, 3/4 product-photography angle, approximately 50mm lens, high detail, photorealistic.

## In-Context Prompt

Photorealistic workshop scene of the G4 duct-flute prototype being voiced, with the removable block partly withdrawn, small shims for windway adjustment, files near the window, and a tuner or validation sheet on the bench. Natural window light, PVC proof body and hardwood blank side by side, careful contemporary workshop interpretation, no cultural ownership claims or maker logos.

## Detail Prompt

Photorealistic macro close-up of the duct-flute mouthpiece: the windway exit, fitted removable block, rectangular window, crisp labium ramp, and bore interior just visible, with a few undersized tone holes fading into the background. Shallow depth of field, high detail, realistic PVC or wood texture and fine filed edges.

## Negative / Avoid

Avoid text, watermarks, logos, panpipe bundles, recorder beaks unrelated to the block-and-window design, impossible tone-hole spacing, extra limbs, distorted hands, and fake tuning labels.
