# High Pass Morning

**Tradition/source:** Tony Koop / Heifer Zephyr (2026)
**Public domain:** Yes - pre-1929 US or original MIT
**Arrangement:** Starter L2 arrangement for `andean-duct-flutes`.

## Range check
G4-G5: within the beginner starter range for this packet.

## Practice tip
Aim for a bright, direct tone and keep the repeated Gs buoyant.

## Known gaps
